About Fikadu Lemmessa

Fikadu Lemmessa is born and raised in central Ethiopia. He have BSc in Agricultural Science from Mekelle University and MSc in Geoinformation Science from Addis Ababa University. Forthe past 5 years,he had been working as geospatial analyst in areas of geospatial data collection, processing, analyzing and visualization. He had been working on a variety of geospatial application areas like basemap preparation for urban planning, cadastral data preparation, land suitability analysis, road network analysis and mapping as technical analyst and project manager positions. Currently he is pursuing Masters of Science in Geospatial Technologies at University of Washington Tacoma.
